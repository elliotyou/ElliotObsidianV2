to cause or allow something to continue for a period of time  
保持，維持，使持續，使繼續 

>The economy looks set to sustain its growth into next year.  
>看來經濟增長會持續到明年 

>He seems to find it difficult to sustain relationships with women. 
>他似乎覺得和女人維持關係並不容易。
