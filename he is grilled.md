### he is grilled (v)\[T\]  
/ɡrɪl/  (QUESTION)  
to ask someone a lot of questions for a long time 
盤問，審問 
>After being grilled by the police for two days, Johnson signed a confession. 
>被警方審問了兩天後，約翰遜在供狀上簽字認罪。 

>Her parents would grill her about where she'd been. 
>她父母會盤問她到哪裡去了。
