### subsidy (n)
/ˈsʌb.sə.di/
money given as part of the cost of something, to help or encourage it to happen 
補助金；津貼，補貼

>The company received a substantial government subsidy. 
>公司得到大筆政府津貼。 

>The government is planning to abolish subsidies to farmers. 
>政府正計劃取消對農場主人的補貼。
