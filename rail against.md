### rail against  (v)[I]  
/reɪl/  formal 

to complain angrily 
譴責；抱怨 

>He railed against/at the injustices of the system. 
>他強烈譴責該制度的不公正。  

>He(Trump) took the opportunity to rail against both protesters and statues being torn down across America. 
>他藉機批評抗議人士以及美國各地的雕像被推倒。
